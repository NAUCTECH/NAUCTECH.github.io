    Announcements:

    • All of the computer science classes at the Armory are free and open to the public. The classes are focused on students grades 2-8. Our third cohort will start on March 21st, 2016.
      If you are interested in enrolling your child in one of our free courses please contact Lissette by phone at (845)245-4035

    • The MakerSpace is open to anyone while classes are going on. Your child can have fun learning robotics with LegoMindstorms or Dash and Dot Robots. They can learn electrical engineering with Arduinos, Raspberry Pis, or Snap Circuits. These are just a few things that your child can do in our MakerSpace.


    Dash and Dot Progress

    Grade Levels: 2-4: The students have been making great progress on programming their Dash robots. The recently made their robots drive around on an obstical course made from painters tape that required them to shoot a ball into a hoop at the end. They have learned about loops, and conditional statements and will be using those new found skills in the coming weeks to solve new challenges. In the future, the students will learn how to use the launcher to shoot balls at targets. They will also learn to play music using the dash robots using the music accessories.



    Lego Mindstorm Progress

    Grade Levels: 5-8: The students are having a great time making their Mindstorms drive through and given obstical course while picking up blocks and placing them in at different, predefined, locations. Some of the students made short work of that so we asked them to program the robot to do it all backwards and reset the board. They had some trouble doing this, but did a great job working through the problems and some eventually solved the challenge!!
